About the Archive

A dignified public record

This website is being developed as a free static legacy archive. Its purpose is to preserve approved, verified, and permission-cleared materials for family members, community organisations, researchers, and future generations.

The archive should remain factual, respectful, and evidence-based. It is intended for preservation rather than exaggeration.

Contribute a Story

Help preserve the record

Persons wishing to contribute a short video, written reflection, photograph, or document should provide the contributor name, relationship to Brother Harry, date, location, and permission for use.

All submitted materials should be reviewed before publication to confirm accuracy, consent, and suitability for the public archive.

Archive Notice

Publication and preservation standards

  • Publish only approved and permission-cleared materials.
  • Keep private family records, raw recordings, and consent forms outside the public website.
  • Maintain transcripts and captions wherever possible.
  • Preserve original files in offline backups as well as online storage.
