About the Archive

A dignified public record for Bro. Harry.

This website is being developed as a free static legacy archive. Its purpose is to preserve approved, verified, and permission-cleared materials for family members, community organisations, researchers, and future generations.

The archive follows a calm, professional spiritual design direction grounded in peace, leadership, service, and Caribbean warmth. It is intended for preservation, public memory, and respectful education.

Contribute a Story

Help preserve the record with care.

Persons wishing to contribute a short video, written reflection, photograph, or document should provide their name, relationship to Bro. Harry, date, location, short description, and permission for use.

All submitted materials should be reviewed before publication to confirm accuracy, dignity, consent, and suitability for the public archive.

Archive Standards

Factual. Dignified. Verifiable. Respectful.

  • Publish only approved, verified, and permission-cleared materials.
  • Keep private family records, consent forms, raw recordings, and identity documents outside the public website.
  • Maintain titles, dates, contributor names, captions, transcripts, and source notes where possible.
  • Use calm language. Avoid exaggerated claims, promotional wording, or unsupported statements.
  • Preserve original files in offline backups as well as online storage.
