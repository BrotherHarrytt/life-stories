About Bro. Harry

A life of spiritual leadership, mediation, and community service.

Harrypersad Maharaj, widely known as Bro. Harry, is a Trinidad and Tobago civil society leader, Justice of the Peace, mediator, counsellor, interfaith representative, humanitarian, and spiritual teacher with decades of national and international service.

He is recognised for leadership in interfaith relations, mediation, humanitarian outreach, spiritual education, counselling, youth empowerment, and social development initiatives throughout Trinidad and Tobago and internationally.

This website is being developed as a living public profile and approved story collection. It is not a memorial page. It is intended to document Bro. Harry's continuing contribution, public service, values, and community impact with dignity and accuracy.

Life Work

Core areas of contribution

Bro. Harry's public contribution is presented through verified, respectful, and service-centred themes.

01

PRERNA Trinidad and Tobago

Founder and Chief Executive Officer of PRERNA Trinidad and Tobago, supporting counselling, mediation, spiritual guidance, community outreach, and humanitarian service.

02

Brahma Kumaris Service

One of the earliest and longest-serving members of the Brahma Kumaris in the Caribbean region, associated with teaching, counselling, youth development, administration, and public representation.

03

Interfaith Leadership

Long-standing service with the Inter Religious Organisation of Trinidad and Tobago, including leadership roles and participation in national prayer, dialogue, youth, and crime-prevention initiatives.

04

Mediation and Peacebuilding

Public and private work in mediation, counselling, conflict resolution, social healing, and national dialogue, including involvement in prominent mediation contexts.

Teenage years

Gandhi, yoga, and personal discipline

June 1977

Featured life moment

Touch by the Light - June 1977

In June 1977, Bro. Harry encountered Dadi Prakashmani, then Administrative Head of the Brahma Kumaris World Spiritual University, during her international visit to Trinidad and Tobago. This experience became a defining spiritual turning point and marked the beginning of his long-standing association with the Brahma Kumaris.

This moment is remembered in the story video titled Touch by the Light.
